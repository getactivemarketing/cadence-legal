Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my coach? Go to Settings and tap your current coach to select a new one.
How do I log a workout? Tap the "Log Workout" button on the home screen, add exercises and sets, then save.
How do I delete my account? Go to Settings, scroll to the bottom, and tap "Delete Account." This permanently removes all your data.
Is my data private? Yes. Your data is stored securely and never shared with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.
